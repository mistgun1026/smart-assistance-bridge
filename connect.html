Connecting to QuickBooks Online

Smart Assistance Bridge runs locally on the operator's own computer. Connecting is done from that machine, not from this page.

  1. Open a terminal in the Smart Assistance Bridge folder.
  2. Run the authorization command. A browser window opens on Intuit's sign-in page.
  3. Sign in with the QuickBooks account that owns or administers the company file.
  4. Review the permissions Intuit lists, then approve.
  5. You are returned to a confirmation page and the connection is stored locally.

Only a QuickBooks account holder or an administrator can authorize the connection. Access is limited to the single company file selected during sign-in.

To connect a different company, repeat the same steps and select that company at sign-in.
