You have been disconnected

Smart Assistance Bridge no longer has access to your QuickBooks Online company. No further data will be read or written.

To disconnect at any time

  1. Sign in to QuickBooks Online.
  2. Go to Settings → Apps (or visit your Intuit account's connected apps page).
  3. Find Smart Assistance Bridge in the list.
  4. Choose Disconnect.

Access is revoked immediately. The stored token on the operator's computer stops working at that moment.

Because the Application keeps no copy of your QuickBooks data, there is nothing further to delete on our side. If you would like written confirmation of that, email us.
