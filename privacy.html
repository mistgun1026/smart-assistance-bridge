Privacy Policy

Smart Assistance Bridge · Last updated: 20 July 2026

1. Who we are

Smart Assistance Bridge is operated as an internal tool. You can reach us at samsonbenzar1026@gmail.com.

2. What information we access

When the Application is connected to QuickBooks Online, it may access:

  • Company information (name, address, settings)
  • Customer and vendor records
  • Transactions: invoices, bills, payments, deposits, journal entries
  • Chart of accounts and balances
  • Financial reports such as Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet
  • Employee records, where covered by the permissions granted

We do not access your QuickBooks password. The connection uses OAuth 2.0: Intuit issues an access token, and your login credentials never pass through the Application.

3. How we use it

Data is used only to respond to requests made by the operator inside the Application — for example, producing a report or looking up a transaction.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it to train any model.

4. Where data is stored

The Application runs locally on the operator's own computer. Access tokens are stored in a local configuration file on that machine. No QuickBooks data is stored on our servers, because we operate no server for this purpose.

5. Retention

We do not retain a copy of your QuickBooks data. Each request is fetched fresh from Intuit's API and discarded after the response is produced.

6. Third parties

The Application communicates with:

  • Intuit / QuickBooks Online — the source of the data
  • Anthropic (Claude) — processes the operator's questions and the data needed to answer them

No other third party receives your data.

7. Your rights

You may revoke access at any time in your Intuit account settings, request deletion of any data we hold, or request a copy of it. Contact samsonbenzar1026@gmail.com.

8. Children's data

The Application is intended for business use and is not directed to anyone under 18.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The date above shows when it was last revised.
