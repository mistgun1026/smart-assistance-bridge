Smart Assistance Bridge

An internal accounting assistant that connects to QuickBooks Online.

Smart Assistance Bridge is a private, internal-use tool. It connects to a QuickBooks Online company through Intuit's official API so that authorized staff can look up records and generate reports using plain-language questions.

It runs locally on the operator's own computer. It is not a hosted service, it is not sold, and it is not available to the general public.

Smart Assistance Bridge is not affiliated with or endorsed by Intuit Inc.
